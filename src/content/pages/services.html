Frequently Asked Questions

If you don't find an answer to your questions about our features or services, please reach out to us, we're happy to help.

  • SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization. It refers to the practice of optimizing your website to increase its visibility and ranking in search engine results pages (SERPs). When people search for keywords or phrases related to your business, you want your website to appear as high up in the search results as possible. This is important because the higher your website ranks, the more likely it is that users will click on it, leading to increased traffic and potential customers for your business.

  • Technical SEO: For this aspect we focus on optimizing the technical elements of your website, such as site speed, mobile-friendliness, site structure, and schema markup, to ensure it is easily accessible and indexable by search engines.
    On-Page Optimization: This includes optimizing various on-page elements such as title tags, meta descriptions, headings, and images to make them more search engine-friendly.
    Keyword Optimization: This involves researching and selecting relevant keywords that users are likely to search for. These keywords are then strategically incorporated into your website's content, meta tags, headings, and URLs to signal to search engines what your website is about.
    Content Optimization: Creating high-quality, informative, and engaging content is crucial for SEO. Search engines prioritize websites that provide valuable information to users. This includes content that is relevant to your business, such as blog posts, news articles, and product descriptions.

  • Off-page optimization refers to the activities and strategies undertaken outside of your website to improve its visibility and authority in search engine results pages (SERPs). While on-page optimization focuses on optimizing the technical elements of your website, off-page optimization involves building your website's reputation and credibility across the internet. Some examples of off-page optimization include:Link Building, Social Media Engagement, Online Reputation Management, Guest Blogging and Content Contributions, and Influencer Outreach and Collaboration.

  • Responsive web design is an approach to designing and building websites that ensures optimal viewing and interaction across a wide range of devices and screen sizes, from desktop computers to smartphones and tablets. A responsively designed website automatically adjusts its layout, content, and functionality based on the device's screen size and orientation, providing users with a seamless and consistent browsing experience.

  • Continuous development is an iterative approach to website development where we perform maintenance and updates and add new features, enhancements, and improvements. Basically, we are always looking for something we can improve on to make your website better.

  • If There is a problem that is having an immediate negative effect on your business, we want to know about it as soon as possible, any time of day or night. For any non-emergency issues after hours please leave or send us a message and we will start on it first thing in the morning.

  • Having a Google Business Profile is crucial for local SEO (Search Engine Optimization) and ensuring that potential customers in your area can easily find and connect with your business. Here's how it helps:
    Increased Visibility: When someone searches for a product or service related to your business in your area, Google often displays a list of local businesses in the search results. A well-optimized Google Business Profile increases the chances of your business appearing in these local search results.
    Map Listings: Your Google Business Profile information is displayed prominently in Google Maps, making it easy for nearby customers to locate your business and get directions.
    Customer Reviews: A Google Business Profile allows customers to leave reviews and ratings about their experience with your business. Positive reviews not only build trust with potential customers but also contribute to your business's visibility in local search results.
    Key info at a glanceYour profile provides essential information such as your business hours, phone number, website, and address. This quick access to information makes it convenient for potential customers to contact or visit your business.
    And, of course, it links directly to your new website
