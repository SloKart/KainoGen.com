Our Process from Start to Finish

What steps we take to build your website

  • 01

    Contact us Let us know what you're looking for and what questions you have.

  • 02

    Let's chat We'll meet up either in person or online and create a plan together for your project. If you like it, we'll sign you up and give you some homework.

  • 03

    Do your homework Don't worry, we won't be grading it. So have some fun. Take a few pictures, or a video. Write some content for us base our copywriting and SEO optimization off off.

  • 04

    Working Together We want you to stand out in the crowd. Send us any photos, logos, or other design elements you would like use or be inspired by.

  • 05

    Bring your ideas to life We'll Design your unique website and create content that will make you stand out to customers and search engines.

  • 06

    Making sure your happy We'll review your design draft with you and make any adjustments you may want.

  • 07

    Writing the code We'll begin coding and testing your website.

  • 08

    Deploy Your new website is live on the internet for the whole world see. Congratulations, you did it.
